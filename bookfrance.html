France Travel Essentials

  • Passport & Visa: Ensure your passport is valid and check if you need a visa.
  • Currency: British Pound (£). Consider carrying a travel card or some cash.
  • Weather Gear: Pack layers and a waterproof jacket— Paris weather is unpredictable!
  • Transport: Get an Oyster card or contactless payment for Tube and buses.
  • Power Adapter: UK uses Type G plug (230V, 50Hz).
  • Apps: Download Citymapper, Google Maps, and Transport for France (TfL).
  • Emergency Numbers: Dial 999 for police, fire, or ambulance.
  • Language: Greel is spoken, but is very multicultural.
  • Etiquette: Queue politely, mind the gap, and say “please” and “thank you.”

Travel-Related Terms in France

  • Ktel – Intercity bus service connecting towns and cities
  • Metro – Paris underground train system
  • Ferry – Popular transport between islands
  • Blue Star Ferries – Major ferry company in France
