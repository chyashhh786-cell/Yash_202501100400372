Dubai Travel Essentials

  • Visa Requirements: Check if you need a visa before arrival. Many nationalities get visa on arrival.
  • Currency: UAE Dirham (AED). Credit cards are widely accepted.
  • Weather: Hot and sunny most of the year. Best time to visit: November to March.
  • Dress Code: Modest clothing is recommended in public places. Swimwear is fine at beaches and pools.
  • Transport: Metro, taxis, and ride-hailing apps like Uber and Careem are popular.
  • Language: Arabic is the official language, but English is widely spoken.
  • Safety: Dubai is very safe for tourists. Follow local laws and customs.

Travel-Related Terms in Dubai

  • Nol Card – Smart card used for public transport (metro, buses, trams)
  • Dubai Metro – Rapid transit system with Red and Green lines
  • Abra – Traditional wooden boat used to cross Dubai Creek
  • RTA – Roads and Transport Authority, manages public transport
