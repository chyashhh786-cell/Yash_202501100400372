London Travel Essentials

  • Passport & Visa: Ensure your passport is valid and check if you need a visa.
  • Currency: British Pound (£). Consider carrying a travel card or some cash.
  • Weather Gear: Pack layers and a waterproof jacket— London weather is unpredictable!
  • Transport: Get an Oyster card or contactless payment for Tube and buses.
  • Power Adapter: UK uses Type G plug (230V, 50Hz).
  • Apps: Download Citymapper, Google Maps, and Transport for London (TfL).
  • Emergency Numbers: Dial 999 for police, fire, or ambulance.
  • Language: English is spoken, but London is very multicultural.
  • Must-See: Big Ben, Tower of London, British Museum, Buckingham Palace.
  • Etiquette: Queue politely, mind the gap, and say “please” and “thank you.”

Travel-Related Terms in London

  • Oyster Card – Contactless travel card for public transport
  • Tube – London Underground metro system
  • Double-Decker Bus – Iconic red buses used for city transport
  • Black Cab – Traditional London taxi
  • Zone 1 – Central London fare zone
